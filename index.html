Places to visit in Bakhmut:

Salt Mines

Located in Soledar, the largest salt deposit in Ukraine and Europe. 125 mi (201 km) of tunnels at a depth of 288 m (945 ft).There are accommodation areas and even a church.

"Toptalovka"

Toptalovka can be translated as "An area where you walk". The city's most important and solemn events take place here: parades, celebrations and art exhibitions. This was previously the burial place of Germans killed in World War II, whose remains were recovered to be transported back to their homeland.

Rose Alley

Bakhmut was awarded the title "City of Roses" in 2015 for setting the Ukrainian record for "Highest Number of Roses per Location". Representatives of the Register of Records recorded 5,091 rose bushes.
